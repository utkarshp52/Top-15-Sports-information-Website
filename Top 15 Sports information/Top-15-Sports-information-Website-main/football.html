Football

Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is the most widely played and followed sport on the planet. It is governed globally by FIFA and played professionally in virtually every country.

The game's simple rules and minimal equipment requirements make it accessible to people of all backgrounds. Football matches are played between two teams of 11 players on a rectangular field with goals at each end. The objective is to score by getting the ball into the opposing team's goal.

Major football tournaments include the FIFA World Cup, held every four years, and club competitions like the UEFA Champions League, English Premier League, La Liga, and many others. The World Cup final is often one of the most-watched sporting events globally.

Countries like Brazil, Germany, Argentina, France, and Italy have a rich footballing history, producing legendary players like Pelé, Diego Maradona, Lionel Messi, and Cristiano Ronaldo.
