Shooting

Shooting is a precision sport involving various types of guns such as rifles and pistols. It is featured in the Olympics and other international competitions. The sport emphasizes concentration, discipline, and accuracy.

India has produced top shooters like Abhinav Bindra (India's first individual Olympic gold medalist), Gagan Narang, and Manu Bhaker.

The International Shooting Sport Federation (ISSF) governs global shooting competitions.
