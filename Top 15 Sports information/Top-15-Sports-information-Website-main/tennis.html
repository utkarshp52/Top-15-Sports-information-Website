Tennis

Tennis is a popular racket sport played one-on-one (singles) or between two teams of two (doubles). Played on grass, clay, or hard courts, the sport is well-known for its prestigious tournaments: Wimbledon, US Open, Australian Open, and French Open.

It has a global following and legendary players like Roger Federer, Serena Williams, Rafael Nadal, and Novak Djokovic.

Tennis is governed internationally by the ITF and featured in the Olympics as well.
