Carrom

Carrom is a popular indoor tabletop game played widely in South Asia. Players flick a striker disk to pocket small disks (carrom men) into corners, aiming for skillful shots and strategic play.

It's played at casual and competitive levels, with national and international championships organized by federations like the International Carrom Federation.
