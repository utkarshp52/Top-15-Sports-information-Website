Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of 11 players. It is most popular in countries like India, England, Australia, and Pakistan. The sport features multiple formats, including Test, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20).

The ICC Cricket World Cup and the Indian Premier League (IPL) are among the biggest cricketing events in the world, drawing huge TV audiences and fan engagement.

Famous cricketers include Sachin Tendulkar, Virat Kohli, M.S. Dhoni, and Ricky Ponting.
