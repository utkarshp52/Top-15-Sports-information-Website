Volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport played with six players on each side, aiming to score points by grounding the ball on the opponent’s court. It is played indoors and on beaches (Beach Volleyball).

The sport is particularly popular in countries like Brazil, USA, Italy, and Russia. It has been a part of the Olympic Games since 1964 (indoor) and 1996 (beach).

The Fédération Internationale de Volleyball (FIVB) is the sport’s governing body.
