Kho-Kho

Kho-Kho is a traditional Indian tag sport that emphasizes speed, agility, and teamwork. It is played by two teams of nine players on a rectangular field. One team chases, while the other tries to avoid being touched.

It is commonly played in schools and colleges across India and has a governing body, the Kho Kho Federation of India. Recent national-level leagues and championships have given the sport renewed visibility.
