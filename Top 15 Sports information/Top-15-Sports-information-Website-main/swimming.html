Swimming

Swimming is both a recreational and competitive sport involving moving through water using the limbs. It's one of the core Olympic sports, with events in various styles such as freestyle, breaststroke, and butterfly.

Top swimmers include Michael Phelps, Katie Ledecky, and Caeleb Dressel. Swimming is governed globally by World Aquatics (formerly FINA).
