Table Tennis

Table Tennis, also known as ping pong, is a fast-paced indoor sport played by two or four players. Originating in England in the late 19th century, it is now immensely popular in countries like China, Japan, and Germany.

It is an Olympic sport and governed internationally by the International Table Tennis Federation (ITTF). Matches are known for their speed, reflexes, and precision.

Top players include Ma Long, Ding Ning, and Timo Boll.
