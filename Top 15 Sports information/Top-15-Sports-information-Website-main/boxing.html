Boxing

Boxing is a combat sport with two opponents fighting in a ring under a set of rules. It is known for intense physical and mental stamina. The sport has a long history and is a staple at the Olympic Games.

India has produced notable boxers like Mary Kom, Vijender Singh, and Lovlina Borgohain.
