Hockey

Hockey, particularly field hockey, holds historical significance in countries like India and Pakistan. It was once considered India's national sport, with a legacy of Olympic victories. The game is played between two teams of 11 players using curved sticks and a ball.

India has a strong legacy in field hockey, with legends like Dhyan Chand. The FIH (International Hockey Federation) governs the sport globally.
